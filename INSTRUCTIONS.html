Part 1: Host Your App (One-Time Setup)

The app needs to be hosted on a website so your phone can open it. The easiest free option is GitHub Pages:

1
Create a free GitHub account
Go to github.com and sign up for a free account. Use any email address.
2
Create a new repository
Click the green New button. Name it dentonfarm. Check Public. Click Create repository.
3
Upload your app files
Click uploading an existing file. Drag and drop all 3 files from the zip you downloaded: index.html, manifest.json, sw.js. Click Commit changes.
4
Enable GitHub Pages
Go to your repository → Settings → Pages (left sidebar) → Under "Source", select Deploy from a branch → Choose main branch → Click Save.
5
Get your app URL
Wait 2–3 minutes, then refresh the Settings → Pages page. Your URL will be: https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/dentonfarm. This is your farm app address!

Part 2: Install on Your Android Phone

1
Open Chrome on your phone
Make sure you're using Google Chrome (not Samsung Internet or Firefox). It's the red/yellow/green/blue circle icon.
2
Go to your app URL
Type or paste your URL into Chrome's address bar: https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/dentonfarm
3
Add to Home Screen
Tap the three dots menu (⋮) in the top right of Chrome → Tap "Add to Home screen" → Tap "Add". The app icon will appear on your home screen!
4
Open the app
Tap the 🌾 Denton South Farm icon on your home screen. It opens fullscreen like a real app — no browser bars!
⚠️ Allow camera access: The first time you tap "Scan Receipt," Chrome will ask permission to use your camera. Tap Allow so the receipt scanner works.

Part 3: Connect Google Sheets (Recommended)

Step A: Create Your Spreadsheet

1
Go to Google Sheets
On your phone or computer, go to sheets.google.com and sign in with your Google account.
2
Create a new spreadsheet
Tap the + button to create a blank spreadsheet. Rename it "Denton South Farm" by clicking the title at top.

Step B: Set Up the Apps Script (for auto-sync)

1
Open Apps Script
In your Google Sheet, click Extensions → Apps Script (do this on a computer, not phone).
2
Paste the script
Delete everything in the editor. Open the file google-apps-script.js from your download, copy all the text, and paste it into the Apps Script editor.
3
Deploy as Web App
Click Deploy → New deployment → Click the gear icon and choose Web app → Set "Execute as" to Me → Set "Who has access" to Anyone → Click Deploy → Copy the Web app URL.
4
Connect in your app
In the farm app, tap Settings → Google Sheets Sync → Paste the Web app URL in the "Apps Script Web App URL" box → Tap Save & Connect.

Troubleshooting

ProblemSolution
Camera doesn't workIn Chrome, go to Settings → Site Settings → Camera → find your app URL → Allow
Receipt scan failsMake sure the receipt is well-lit and in focus. You can also edit items manually after scanning.
Data disappearedData is stored locally in Chrome. Don't clear Chrome's browsing data/cache. Google Sheets sync is your backup!
Google Sync failsMake sure you deployed the Apps Script as "Anyone" can access. Re-deploy if needed.
App is slowClose other apps on your phone. The receipt scanner requires internet for AI analysis.

Your Data is Safe

  • All data saves automatically to your phone (even offline)
  • Google Sheets sync creates a backup in your Google account
  • Use "Export CSV" anytime for a local backup you can open in Excel
  • Receipt images stay on your device; they are sent to AI for analysis but not stored
📞 Need help? The app was built specifically for Denton South Farm. All your data belongs to you and stays in your Google account.
